Web Develpment Foundations

In this course, students learn design and computer science fundamentals by coding web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Each language receives its own focus and students will gain a working understanding of each of them. It is designed to be taught over the course of a semester. Any internet-enabled computer with a keyboard, mouse, and Google Chrome installed will work for this course.
